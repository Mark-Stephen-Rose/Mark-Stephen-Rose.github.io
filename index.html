About Me

I care about using data to make honest, useful decisions — and about doing it responsibly.

I'm a data science major and business minor at Stonehill College, working at the intersection of statistics, engineering, and real-world impact. I work primarily in Python, R, SQL, and Power BI, and I'm especially drawn to problems where clean data pipelines directly translate into better decisions.

I'm currently looking for internships and full-time opportunities in data science, where I can keep building things that matter — from clinical trial dashboards to machine learning models — while staying mindful of the ethical questions data work always raises.

Outside of work, I'm usually golfing, fishing, playing guitar, or planning the next trip. I'm always happy to talk shop or swap recommendations — feel free to reach out.
